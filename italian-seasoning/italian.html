Italina seasoning

This Italian seasoning blend is easy and quick to throw together. This is an excellent recipe for all your Italian seasoning needs. I like to use it for just about everything, Italian or not. Stored in an airtight container, it keeps for about 12 weeks. You can use fresh herbs you dry yourself, or store-bought dried herbs â€” of course, the ones you grow and dry yourself are better!

Ingredients for Italina seasoning

  • 4 tablespoons dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons dried rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons dried marjoram
  • 2 tablespoons dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons red pepper flakes
